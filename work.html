Kinzoo

2020-2021

UX Lead

At Kinzoo I was in charge of updates and improvements for three core apps the company has: Kinzoo Messenger, Together and Kinzoo Adventures. For the latest one I participated in the first iterations, while also defined the art direction, was in charge of hiring of illustrators and animators, defined the deliverables roadmap and also managed the whole creative team.

For Together, I was in charge of bridging this newly aquired app to the Kinzoo branding, while mantaining the team possibilities. For Kinzoo messenger I worked closely with developers and product owners to publish the newest features and iterations of the ipad, ios and android apps.

Freelance

2018-2020

Branding, UX/UI

In my freelance years I explored a wide variety of projects, I had the opportunity to work doing illustrations, brand design, but also websites and product design. I enjoy this ability to switch according to the need, and honestly I usually get quite bored if I’m only doing the same thing for a long period of time.

I believe the world is big, and there’s a wide vast ocean of companies and people with design needs, I enjoy this bridge the profession offers to people like me, where we can learn and get involved in areas we would have never thought of otherwise. It’s one of the things I love the most about being a designer.
